$$ \mathbf{E} \approx \frac{1}{4 \pi \epsilon_0 r^3} \Big( \frac{3\mathbf{p \cdot r}}{r^2} \mathbf{r-p} \Big) $$ $$ V \approx \frac{|q|xd}{4 \pi \epsilon_0 r^3} $$
where $ \mathbf{p} = q\mathbf{d} $ is the dipole moment,
$ \mathbf{d} $ is the vector pointing from negative charge to positive charge,
and $ x $ is the $ x $-coordinate of the point from the origin.
